how to make pasta for two

from scratch

Embark on a culinary journey by crafting fresh pasta from scratch, tailored perfectly for two. This guide simplifies the traditional Italian method, eliminating the need for specialized equipment. With just a few basic ingredients—flour, eggs, and a pinch of salt—you can create a delightful homemade meal.

Whether you're aiming to impress a loved one or enjoy a cozy dinner together, this recipe provides step-by-step instructions to achieve authentic, restaurant-quality pasta in the comfort of your kitchen.

ingredients and tools

  • 200g “00” tipo flour
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • Rolling pin
  • Pizza cutter

step 01

make the dough

  • on a clean surface, make a mound of flour with a well in the center.
  • add the eggs into the well. add a pinch of salt if using.
  • use a fork to slowly mix the eggs, pulling in flour from the edges.
  • when it thickens, knead with your hands until smooth (8–10 mins).
  • wrap in plastic and rest for 30 mins.

step 02

roll

  • cut dough in half. keep unused half covered.
  • flour your surface and roll dough into a thin sheet (about 1–2mm thick).
  • and flour as needed to prevent sticking.

step 03

fold

  • starting at one edge, fold the sheet over itself loosely, like folding a letter.
  • repeat this folding process until the entire sheet is folded into a flattened roll.

step 04

cut

  • cut 1/4” pieces with a pizza cutter.
  • make sure you apply enough pressure to cut through the dough.

step 05

boil and enjoy

  • boil pasta 3-5 minutes until al dente.
  • toss in you favorite sauce and enjoy!
