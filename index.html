Past Favorite Projects

Here you will find some of my personal favorite projects. These were done this past Winter Quarter, with them all being branding.


Muush Brand Identity

Muush is an imaginative mushroom oatmeal brand that was brought to life. While "mushroom oatmeal" might not sound exciting, the playful name "Muush," with its double U's, adds a fun twist. Staying true to the brand’s organic roots, infusing it with vibrant colors and whimsical mushroom illustrations, gives this nature-driven concept a lively and approachable personality that will stand out on the shelves.


El Capitan's Rebrand

El Capitan’s is your ultimate hangout in downtown Bellingham, WA—offering everything from drinks and hot dogs to trivia nights and live music. This lively spot embraces a bold pirate theme that adds a touch of adventure to the atmosphere. My approach keeps that rebellious, pirate-inspired spirit alive, blending gritty language with the energy of the local nightlife in this charming college town.


Midnight Coffee Co. Logo Design

This fictional brand is designed with night owls in mind, embracing the spirit of the evening hours. Featuring the Nina typeface by Taylor Penton Designs, it combines a hand-drawn quality with a modular structure, striking a perfect balance between personal touch and versatility. Complemented by hand-drawn elements, the brand exudes a unique, organic feel that captures the essence of creativity and nocturnal energy.
